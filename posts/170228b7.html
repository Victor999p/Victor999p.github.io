Ceramic and Metal Biomaterials: Types, Manufacturing, and Applications | 陶瓷与金属生物材料：类型、制造与应用

Ceramic | 陶瓷

Ceramic Types & Manufacturing | 陶瓷类型与制造

  • Traditional Ceramics: Porcelain, Stoneware, Earthenware; each uses unique chemical compositions and raw materials. The Hong Kong/Canton region is known for specialized techniques.
    传统陶瓷： 瓷器、炻器、陶器；各自采用独特的化学成分和原材料。港粤地区以专门工艺著称。
  • Manufacturing Process: Clay (powder + water) is shaped, dried (shrinks and reorganizes), then fired/sintered to bond particles.
    制造过程： 粘土（粉末+水）成型、干燥（收缩与重组）、烧结使颗粒结合。
  • Materials: Kaolinite and Bentonite clays; Flint and Quartz additives reduce shrinkage; Feldspar creates glossy, non-porous finishes.
    原材料： 高岭土和膨润土；燧石和石英添加剂减少收缩；长石形成光滑致密表面。

Biomedical Ceramic Classifications | 生物医学陶瓷分类

  • Bioactive Ceramics: Bond with tissue (e.g., glass ceramics for implant coatings, stimulate hydroxyapatite formation).
    生物活性陶瓷： 能与组织结合（如用于植入物涂层的玻璃陶瓷，促进羟基磷灰石形成）。
  • Bioresorbable Ceramics: Biodegradable (e.g., calcium phosphate compounds, hydroxyapatite).
    生物可吸收陶瓷： 可降解（如磷酸钙化合物、羟基磷灰石）。
  • Bioinert Ceramics: Minimal tissue interaction (e.g., pyrolytic carbon, alumina for joint replacements and dental crowns).
    生物惰性陶瓷： 与组织相互作用极少（如用于关节置换和牙冠的热解碳、氧化铝）。

Natural Ceramics in the Body | 体内天然陶瓷

  • Bone: ~60% minerals (mainly hydroxyapatite), ~26% collagen, plus cells. Cortical (dense) and trabecular (porous) types; bone marrow contains stem and immune cells.
    骨骼： 约60%矿物质（主要为羟基磷灰石）、约26%胶原蛋白及细胞。分为致密骨和松质骨；骨髓含有干细胞和免疫细胞。
  • Teeth: Higher inorganic content; layers include enamel, dentin, and pulp. Strength from nanostructured calcium phosphate.
    牙齿： 无机物含量更高；分为釉质、牙本质和牙髓。强度来自纳米结构磷酸钙。

Sintering Process & Applications | 烧结工艺与应用

  • Sintering: Occurs at ~⅓ melting point; surface atoms bond without full melting.
    烧结： 在约熔点三分之一温度下进行，表面原子结合但未完全熔化。
  • Applications: Thermal spray coatings, porous structures for cell migration, dental crowns (zirconia vs. alumina), hip implants with hydroxyapatite coatings.
    应用： 热喷涂涂层、细胞迁移的多孔结构、牙冠（氧化锆与氧化铝）、带羟基磷灰石涂层的髋关节植入物。

Overview of Biomedical Materials | 生物医学材料概述

  • Ceramics: Biocompatible, mainly for implant coatings.
    陶瓷： 生物相容性好，主要用于植入物涂层。
  • Metals: High mechanical strength, used for load-bearing implants.
    金属： 机械强度高，用于承重植入物。

Ceramic Manufacturing & Applications | 陶瓷制造与应用

  • Steps: Raw material prep, shaping, drying, sintering.
    步骤： 原料准备、成型、干燥、烧结。
  • Applications: Dental/orthopedic coatings, surface treatments to minimize protein/cell deposition and reduce friction.
    应用： 牙科/骨科涂层，表面处理减少蛋白质/细胞沉积和摩擦。

Metals | 金属

Types & Properties of Medical Metals | 医用金属类型与性质

  • Stainless Steel: Affordable, but poor corrosion resistance.
    不锈钢： 价格低廉，但耐腐蚀性差。
  • Cobalt-Based Alloys: Better corrosion resistance, but potential carcinogenic ion release.
    钴基合金： 耐腐蚀性更好，但可能释放致癌离子。
  • Titanium Alloys: Best biocompatibility and corrosion resistance, non-toxic, but expensive.
    钛合金： 生物相容性和耐腐蚀性最佳，无毒但价格昂贵。

Special Metal Materials | 特殊金属材料

  • Shape Memory Alloys: Nickel-Titanium; used in braces, stents, IVC filters; return to shape when heated.
    形状记忆合金： 镍钛合金；用于牙套、支架、下腔静脉滤器，加热后可恢复原形。
  • Liquid Metals: Solid at room temp, liquid at body temp; used in wearable electronics (e.g., mercury, gallium).
    液态金属： 常温为固体，体温为液体；用于可穿戴电子（如汞、镓）。
  • Biodegradable Metals: Magnesium- and zinc-based; gradually absorbed by the body.
    可降解金属： 镁基和锌基金属，能被人体逐渐吸收。

Corrosion Issues & Protection | 腐蚀问题与防护

  • Mechanism: Oxidation, electrode formation, accelerated by moisture/oxygen.
    机制： 氧化、电极形成，受潮湿/氧气加速。
  • Biological Impact: Corrosion products trigger immune response and inflammation, may cause bone degradation.
    生物影响： 腐蚀产物引发免疫反应和炎症，可能导致骨质破坏。
  • Protection: Use corrosion-resistant metals (chromium, titanium), inert ceramic coatings, and protective oxide layers.
    防护措施： 采用耐腐蚀金属（铬、钛）、惰性陶瓷涂层和保护性氧化层。

Tutorial | 教程

Practice Questions | 练习题

Q1. What is the driving force for the sintering (firing) process of ceramics? | 陶瓷烧结过程的驱动力是什么？

  • A. The high surface free energy under heat and pressure | 高温高压下的高表面自由能
  • B. High pressure in the environment | 环境中的高压力
  • C. Heat (causing the melting of ceramics particles) | 热（导致陶瓷颗粒熔化）
  • D. Ironic attraction | 离子吸引

✅ A. The high surface free energy under heat and pressure
Explanation: Sintering occurs due to the reduction of surface energy, promoting particle bonding under heat.
解析： 烧结是由于表面能降低，在加热下促进颗粒结合。

Q2. What is the difference between bioactive and bioinert ceramics in dental implants? | 牙科植入物中生物活性陶瓷与生物惰性陶瓷的区别是什么？

  • A. Coating of implant; coating for crown | 植入物涂层；牙冠涂层
  • B. Implant; coating for crown | 植入物；牙冠涂层
  • C. Coating of implant; Implant | 植入物涂层；植入物
  • D. Implant; Crown | 植入物；牙冠

✅ C. Coating of implant; Implant
Explanation: Bioactive ceramics are used as coatings to promote bone bonding; bioinert ceramics are used as structural implant materials.
解析： 生物活性陶瓷用于涂层促进骨结合，生物惰性陶瓷用于结构性植入材料。

Q3. What is the chemistry behind the corrosion resistance of titanium dioxide? | 二氧化钛耐腐蚀性的化学原理是什么？

  • A. Titanium dioxide is very chemically stable and does not react with most of the chemicals | 二氧化钛化学性质非常稳定，不与大多数化学物质反应
  • B. It is metal oxide | 它是金属氧化物
  • C. It is white | 它是白色的
  • D. None of the above | 以上都不是

✅ A. Titanium dioxide is very chemically stable and does not react with most of the chemicals
Explanation: TiO₂ forms a passive, stable oxide layer that protects titanium from corrosion.
解析： 二氧化钛形成稳定的钝化氧化层，保护钛免受腐蚀。

Q4. Why is stainless steel still used despite titanium’s superiority? | 尽管钛更优，为什么仍使用不锈钢？

  • A. Cost | 成本
  • B. Accessibility | 易获得性
  • C. Tradition and habit | 传统与习惯
  • D. All of the above | 以上全部

✅ D. All of the above
Explanation: Stainless steel is cheaper, widely available, and historically used in implants.
解析： 不锈钢价格低廉、易获得且有历史使用传统。

Q5. Roles of polymer, ceramic, and metal in full hip replacement | 全髋关节置换中聚合物、陶瓷和金属的作用

  • A. Acetabulum: Polymer; Femoral head: Ceramic; Femoral stem: Metal | 髋臼：聚合物；股骨头：陶瓷；股骨柄：金属
  • B. Acetabulum: Ceramic; Femoral head: Metal; Femoral stem: Polymer | 髋臼：陶瓷；股骨头：金属；股骨柄：聚合物
  • C. Acetabulum: Metal; Femoral head: Polymer; Femoral stem: Ceramic | 髋臼：金属；股骨头：聚合物；股骨柄：陶瓷
  • D. Acetabulum: Ceramic; Femoral head: Ceramic; Femoral stem: Metal | 髋臼：陶瓷；股骨头：陶瓷；股骨柄：金属

✅ D. Acetabulum: Ceramic; Femoral head: Ceramic; Femoral stem: Metal
Explanation: Polymer is used in acetabular liners, ceramic for femoral heads, and metal for femoral stems.
解析： 聚合物用于髋臼衬垫，陶瓷用于股骨头，金属用于股骨柄。

Q6. Dental Implant Design | 牙科植入物设计

  • (4 marks) List two major reasons people choose titanium to make the dental implant (screw). | 列举两大选择钛制作牙科植入物（螺钉）的原因
  • (6 marks) List two benefits of the hydroxyapatite coating. | 列举羟基磷灰石涂层的两大优点

Titanium advantages:

  • Excellent biocompatibility | 优异的生物相容性
  • High corrosion resistance due to stable oxide layer | 稳定氧化层带来高耐腐蚀性

Hydroxyapatite coating benefits:

  • Promotes osseointegration (bone bonding) | 促进骨结合
  • Mimics natural bone mineral, enhancing biological response | 模仿天然骨矿物，增强生物反应

Q7. What is pyrolytic carbon? | 什么是热解碳？

✅ Pyrolytic carbon is a ceramic-like material known for its biocompatibility, chemical inertness, and wear resistance.
Explanation: It is produced by the thermal decomposition of hydrocarbons and is widely used in heart valve prostheses and orthopedic implants due to its durability and compatibility with blood and tissue.
解析： 热解碳是一种类陶瓷材料，具有生物相容性、化学惰性和耐磨性。由碳氢化合物热分解制得，广泛用于心脏瓣膜和骨科植入物，因其耐用且与血液和组织兼容。

Q8. What kind of material is the Simon IVC filter made of? | Simon下腔静脉滤器由什么材料制成？

✅ The Simon IVC filter is made of a shape memory metal, typically nitinol (nickel-titanium alloy).
Explanation: Nitinol provides flexibility, biocompatibility, and self-expanding properties, making it ideal for vascular implants like IVC filters.
解析： Simon下腔静脉滤器由形状记忆金属制成，通常为镍钛合金。镍钛合金具有柔韧性、生物相容性和自扩展性，适合血管植入物。
