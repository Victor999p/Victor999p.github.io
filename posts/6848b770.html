🧬 1. Definition of Polymer | 聚合物的定義

Expanded Explanation | 詳細說明 :
Polymers are macromolecules formed by the covalent linking of smaller repeating units called monomers. These monomers are typically small organic molecules that bond together through chemical reactions like polymerization (e.g., addition or condensation polymerization). Polymers can be classified based on their origin, structure, or properties: | 聚合物是由較小的重複單元（稱為單體）通過共價鍵連接形成的大分子。這些單體通常是小的有機分子，通過聚合反應（如加成聚合或縮合聚合）結合在一起。聚合物可根據其來源、結構或性質進行分類：

  • Natural Polymers: Found in biological systems, such as proteins (chains of amino acids), DNA (nucleotides), and cellulose (glucose units). These polymers are essential for life and often biodegradable. | 天然聚合物：存在於生物系統中，如蛋白質（氨基酸鏈）、DNA（核苷酸）和纖維素（葡萄糖單元）。這些聚合物對生命至關重要，且通常可生物降解。
  • Synthetic Polymers: Human-made, such as polyethylene (used in plastic bags), polystyrene (used in foam packaging), and nylon (used in textiles). These are often designed for specific applications. | 合成聚合物：人造聚合物，如聚乙烯（用於塑料袋）、聚苯乙烯（用於泡沫包裝）和尼龍（用於紡織品）。這些聚合物通常為特定應用而設計。
  • Copolymers: Polymers with two or more different monomer types, arranged in patterns like alternating (A-B-A-B), block (A-A-A-B-B-B), or random (A-B-B-A-B-A). Copolymers allow tailored properties, e.g., styrene-butadiene rubber for tires. | 共聚物：包含兩種或多種不同單體類型的聚合物，排列方式包括交替（A-B-A-B）、嵌段（A-A-A-B-B-B）或隨機（A-B-B-A-B-A）。共聚物可定制性能，例如用於輪胎的苯乙烯-丁二烯橡膠。
  • Molecular Weight: A polymer’s molecular weight, measured in Daltons (Da), reflects the chain length and number of monomers. High molecular weights (25,000–200,000 Da or more) lead to unique properties like elasticity, strength, or viscosity. For example, ultra-high-molecular-weight polyethylene (UHMWPE) is used in joint implants due to its durability. | 分子量：聚合物的分子量以道爾頓（Da）為單位，反映鏈長和單體數量。高分子量（25,000–200,000 Da或更高）帶來獨特的性能，如彈性、強度或黏度。例如，超高分子量聚乙烯（UHMWPE）因其耐用性而用於關節植入物。

Key Insight | 關鍵見解 :
The diversity of monomers and bonding arrangements allows polymers to have a vast range of properties, making them critical in industries from packaging to biomedical engineering. | 單體和鍵結方式的多樣性使聚合物具有廣泛的性能，使其在從包裝到生物醫學工程的各行業中至關重要。

💧 2. Equilibrium Swelling Theory | 平衡膨脹理論

Expanded Explanation | 詳細說明 :
Equilibrium swelling theory explains how hydrogels (polymeric networks that absorb water) reach a balance between swelling and contraction. The Flory–Huggins theory is a cornerstone for understanding this behavior, modeling the thermodynamics of polymer solutions and gels. The swelling process is governed by two opposing forces: | 平衡膨脹理論解釋了水凝膠（吸收水的聚合物網絡）如何在膨脹和收縮之間達到平衡。Flory–Huggins理論是理解此行為的基石，模擬聚合物溶液和凝膠的熱力學。膨脹過程受兩個相反力的控制：

  1. Thermodynamic Mixing Force: This arises from the favorable entropy of mixing water with the polymer network. Water molecules penetrate the network, causing it to expand. The interaction parameter (χ) in Flory–Huggins theory quantifies polymer-solvent compatibility—lower χ values indicate better compatibility, promoting swelling. | 熱力學混合力：這源於水與聚合物網絡混合的有利熵。水分子滲入網絡，導致其膨脹。Flory–Huggins理論中的交互參數（χ）量化了聚合物-溶劑的相容性，較低的χ值表示更好的相容性，促進膨脹。
  2. Elastic Retractive Force: Crosslinked polymer chains resist stretching as the network swells. This elastic force, derived from the entropy of chain conformations, opposes swelling. The extent of crosslinking (how tightly the chains are connected) heavily influences this force. | 彈性回縮力：交聯的聚合物鏈在網絡膨脹時抵抗拉伸。這種彈性力源於鏈構象的熵，反對膨脹。交聯程度（鏈條連接的緊密程度）對此力有很大影響。

Equilibrium State | 平衡狀態 :
Swelling stops when the chemical potential of water inside the gel equals that outside, balancing the mixing and elastic forces. Mathematically, this is described by the Flory–Rehner equation for crosslinked gels, which extends Flory–Huggins theory to account for elasticity. | 當凝膠內水的化學勢與外部相等時，膨脹停止，混合力和彈性力達到平衡。數學上，這由交聯凝膠的Flory–Rehner方程描述，該方程擴展了Flory–Huggins理論以考慮彈性。

Example | 示例 :
A hydrogel used in contact lenses swells to a specific volume based on its polymer composition (e.g., poly(HEMA)) and crosslinking density, ensuring comfort and oxygen permeability. | 用於隱形眼鏡的水凝膠根據其聚合物組成（例如，聚(HEMA)）和交聯密度膨脹到特定體積，確保舒適性和氧氣滲透性。

Key Insight | 關鍵見解 :
The balance of these forces allows hydrogels to be engineered for specific swelling behaviors, critical for applications like drug delivery or tissue scaffolds. | 這些力的平衡使水凝膠能夠被設計為特定的膨脹行為，這對藥物傳遞或組織支架等應用至關重要。

🕸️ 4. Mesh Size | 網孔大小

Expanded Explanation | 詳細說明 :
Mesh size (ξ) is the average distance between crosslinks in a hydrogel’s polymer network, typically on the nanometer scale (1–100 nm). It’s a critical parameter that governs the hydrogel’s physical and functional properties. | 網孔大小（ξ）是水凝膠聚合物網絡中交聯點之間的平均距離，通常在奈米級（1–100奈米）。這是控制水凝膠物理和功能特性的關鍵參數。

Factors Affecting Mesh Size | 影響網孔大小的因素 :

  • Crosslinking Density: Higher crosslinking results in a smaller mesh size, creating a denser network. | 交聯密度：較高的交聯導致較小的網孔大小，形成更密集的網絡。
  • Polymer Chain Length: Longer chains between crosslinks increase mesh size. | 聚合物鏈長度：交聯點之間的較長鏈會增加網孔大小。
  • Swelling State: As the hydrogel swells, the mesh size increases due to chain stretching. | 膨脹狀態：隨著水凝膠膨脹，網孔大小因鏈條拉伸而增加。

Impact of Mesh Size | 網孔大小的影響 :

  1. Diffusion: Smaller mesh sizes restrict the diffusion of larger molecules (e.g., proteins or drugs), while larger mesh sizes allow faster diffusion. For example, insulin (≈6 kDa) diffuses more slowly through a tightly crosslinked gel. | 擴散：較小的網孔大小限制較大分子（如蛋白質或藥物）的擴散，而較大的網孔大小允許更快擴散。例如，胰島素（≈6 kDa）在緊密交聯的凝膠中擴散較慢。
  2. Mechanical Strength: Smaller mesh sizes correlate with higher mechanical strength due to a denser network but may reduce flexibility. | 機械強度：較小的網孔大小與較高的機械強度相關，因網絡更密集，但可能降低柔韌性。
  3. Swelling Behavior: Larger mesh sizes allow more water absorption, increasing the swelling ratio. | 膨脹行為：較大的網孔大小允許更多水吸收，增加膨脹比率。

⚗️ 5. Chemical vs. Physical Hydrogels | 化學與物理水凝膠

Expanded Explanation | 詳細說明 :
Hydrogels are classified based on the type of crosslinking that holds their polymer networks together. The differences between chemical and physical hydrogels impact their properties and applications. | 水凝膠根據維持其聚合物網絡的交聯類型進行分類。化學和物理水凝膠之間的差異影響其性能和應用。

  • Stable under a wide range of conditions, making them suitable for permanent implants or drug delivery systems. | 在廣泛條件下穩定，適用於永久植入物或藥物傳遞系統。

  • Formed by weaker, non-covalent interactions, allowing responsiveness to stimuli (e.g., temperature, pH). | 通過較弱的非共價相互作用形成，允許對刺激（如溫度、pH）的響應。

The choice between chemical and physical hydrogels depends on the application—chemical for stability, physical for responsiveness and reversibility. | 化學與物理水凝膠的選擇取決於應用—化學水凝膠用於穩定性，物理水凝膠用於響應性和可逆性。

🌡️ 6. Lower Critical Solution Temperature (LCST) | 低臨界溶液溫度 (LCST)

Expanded Explanation | 詳細說明 :
The Lower Critical Solution Temperature (LCST) is a critical temperature at which a polymer solution undergoes a phase transition from soluble (miscible with water) to insoluble (phase-separated). Below the LCST, the polymer chains are hydrated and extended; above the LCST, hydrophobic interactions dominate, causing chain collapse and gelation or precipitation. | 低臨界溶液溫度 (LCST) 是聚合物溶液從可溶（與水混溶）轉變為不溶（相分離）的臨界溫度。在LCST以下，聚合物鏈被水合並伸展；在LCST以上，疏水相互作用主導，導致鏈收縮和凝膠化或沉澱。

Mechanism | 機制 :

  • LCST behavior arises from a balance between hydrophilic (e.g., hydrogen bonding) and hydrophobic interactions. | LCST行為源於親水性（例如，氫鍵）和疏水性相互作用的平衡。
  • At low temperatures, hydrogen bonds between polymer and water dominate, keeping the polymer soluble. | 在低溫下，聚合物與水之間的氫鍵主導，使聚合物保持可溶。
  • Above the LCST, entropy-driven hydrophobic interactions cause the polymer to aggregate, expelling water. | 在LCST以上，熵驅動的疏水相互作用導致聚合物聚集成團，排出水。

Example | 示例 :
Poly(N-isopropylacrylamide) (PNIPAM) has an LCST around 32°C, close to body temperature, making it ideal for biomedical applications. Below 32°C, PNIPAM hydrogels are swollen and transparent; above 32°C, they collapse and become opaque, releasing entrapped water or drugs. | 聚(N-異丙基丙烯醯胺) (PNIPAM) 的LCST約為32°C，接近人體溫度，適合生物醫學應用。在32°C以下，PNIPAM水凝膠膨脹且透明；在32°C以上，它們收縮並變得不透明，釋放被困的水或藥物。

Applications | 應用 :

  • Cell Sheet Engineering: PNIPAM-coated surfaces allow cells to grow at 37°C (above LCST) and detach as intact sheets when cooled below 32°C. | 細胞片工程：PNIPAM塗層表面允許細胞在37°C（高於LCST）生長，並在冷卻至32°C以下時以完整片狀脫離。
  • Drug Delivery: LCST-based hydrogels release drugs in response to temperature changes, e.g., at inflammation sites with elevated temperatures. | 藥物傳遞：基於LCST的水凝膠根據溫度變化釋放藥物，例如在溫度升高的炎症部位。

Key Insight | 關鍵見解 :
LCST enables the design of “smart” hydrogels that respond to temperature changes, offering precise control in biomedical and environmental applications. | LCST使“智能”水凝膠的設計成為可能，這些水凝膠可響應溫度變化，在生物醫學和環境應用中提供精確控制。

✅ 7. Advantages of Hydrogels for Biomedical Applications | 水凝膠在生物醫學應用中的優勢

Expanded Explanation | 詳細說明 :
Hydrogels are highly valued in biomedical applications due to their unique properties, which mimic biological tissues and enable tailored functionalities: | 水凝膠因其獨特性能而在生物醫學應用中備受重視，這些性能模擬生物組織並實現定制功能：

  1. High Water Content: Hydrogels can contain 70–99% water, resembling the extracellular matrix (ECM) of tissues. This biomimetic property enhances biocompatibility and reduces immune responses. | 高含水量：水凝膠可含有70–99%的水，類似於組織的細胞外基質（ECM）。這種仿生特性增強了生物相容性並減少免疫反應。
  2. Tunable Properties: Mechanical properties (e.g., stiffness) and chemical properties (e.g., degradation rate) can be adjusted by varying polymer composition, crosslinking density, or functional groups. | 可調性能：機械性能（如剛度）和化學性能（如降解速率）可通過改變聚合物組成、交聯密度或功能基團來調整。
  3. Biocompatibility and Biodegradability: Many hydrogels (e.g., gelatin, hyaluronic acid) are biocompatible and can be designed to degrade naturally in the body, avoiding surgical removal. | 生物相容性和可生物降解性：許多水凝膠（如明膠、透明質酸）具有生物相容性，並可設計為在體內自然降解，避免手術移除。
  4. Stimuli-Responsiveness: Hydrogels can respond to external stimuli like pH, temperature, or enzymes, enabling applications like on-demand drug release. For example, pH-sensitive hydrogels swell in acidic tumor microenvironments to release drugs. | 刺激響應性：水凝膠可響應外部刺激，如pH值、溫度或酶，實現按需藥物釋放等應用。例如，pH敏感水凝膠在酸性腫瘤微環境中膨脹以釋放藥物。
  5. Injectability and In Situ Formation: Some hydrogels (e.g., photo-crosslinkable GelMA) can be injected as liquids and crosslinked in situ, conforming to complex tissue shapes. | 可注射性和原位形成：一些水凝膠（如光交聯GelMA）可作為液體注射並在原位交聯，適應複雜的組織形狀。

Example | 示例 :
Alginate hydrogels are used for wound dressings due to their high water content and ability to maintain a moist healing environment. | 藻酸鹽水凝膠因其高含水量和維持濕潤癒合環境的能力而用於傷口敷料。

Key Insight | 關鍵見解 :
The versatility of hydrogels makes them a cornerstone of modern biomedical engineering, from drug delivery to regenerative medicine. | 水凝膠的多功能性使其成為現代生物醫學工程的基石，從藥物傳遞到再生醫學。

🧪 8. Representative Bioapplications of Hydrogels | 水凝膠的代表性生物應用

Expanded Explanation | 詳細說明 :
Hydrogels are used in a wide range of biomedical applications due to their tunable properties and biocompatibility: | 水凝膠因其可調性能和生物相容性而在廣泛的生物醫學應用中使用：

  1. Drug Delivery Systems: Hydrogels can encapsulate drugs and release them in a controlled manner, either passively (via diffusion) or actively (via stimuli like pH or temperature). | 藥物傳遞系統：水凝膠可包封藥物並以受控方式釋放，通過被動擴散或主動響應（如pH或溫度）。
    • Example: PNIPAM hydrogels release drugs above their LCST in response to body temperature. | 示例：PNIPAM水凝膠在其LCST以上響應體溫釋放藥物。
  2. Tissue Engineering Scaffolds: Hydrogels provide a 3D environment for cell growth, mimicking the ECM. They support cell adhesion, proliferation, and differentiation. | 組織工程支架：水凝膠提供細胞生長的3D環境，模擬ECM，支持細胞粘附、增殖和分化。
    • Example: GelMA (gelatin methacryloyl) scaffolds are used for cartilage or bone regeneration due to their biocompatibility and tunable stiffness. | 示例：GelMA（明膠甲基丙烯酸酯）支架因其生物相容性和可調剛度而用於軟骨或骨再生。
  3. Wound Dressings and Barrier Coatings: Hydrogels maintain a moist environment, promote healing, and can deliver antimicrobial agents. | 傷口敷料和屏障塗層：水凝膠維持濕潤環境，促進癒合，並可傳遞抗菌劑。
    • Example: Alginate-based dressings absorb wound exudate and form a protective gel layer. | 示例：基於藻酸鹽的敷料吸收傷口滲出液並形成保護性凝膠層。
  4. Cell Encapsulation and Immunoisolation: Hydrogels encapsulate cells to protect them from the immune system while allowing nutrient and waste exchange. | 細胞包封和免疫隔離：水凝膠包封細胞以保護其免受免疫系統攻擊，同時允許營養和廢物交換。
    • Example: Alginate microcapsules are used to encapsulate pancreatic islet cells for diabetes treatment, protecting them from immune attack while releasing insulin. | 示例：藻酸鹽微膠囊用於包封胰島細胞以治療糖尿病，保護細胞免受免疫攻擊同時釋放胰島素。

Key Insight | 關鍵見解 :
Hydrogels’ ability to mimic biological environments and respond to physiological cues makes them indispensable in advanced therapeutics and tissue repair. | 水凝膠模擬生物環境並響應生理信號的能力使其在先進治療和組織修復中不可或缺。

🔄 9. Degradation of Hydrogels | 水凝膠的降解

Expanded Explanation | 詳細說明 :
Hydrogel degradation is critical for applications where temporary function is desired, such as drug delivery or tissue scaffolds. Degradation occurs through several mechanisms: | 水凝膠降解對於需要臨時功能的應用（如藥物傳遞或組織支架）至關重要。降解通過以下幾種機制發生：

  1. Backbone Cleavage: The polymer backbone breaks down via hydrolysis, enzymatic cleavage, or oxidative degradation. For example, poly(lactic acid) (PLA) degrades via hydrolysis into lactic acid. | 主鏈斷裂：聚合物主鏈通過水解、酶切或氧化降解斷裂。例如，聚乳酸（PLA）通過水解降解為乳酸。
  2. Crosslinker Breakdown: Crosslinks (covalent or non-covalent) can be designed to degrade under specific conditions, such as pH or enzymes. For example, peptide-based crosslinks are cleaved by matrix metalloproteinases (MMPs). | 交聯劑分解：交聯（共價或非共價）可設計為在特定條件下（如pH或酶）降解。例如，基於肽的交聯被基質金屬蛋白酶（MMPs）切斷。
  3. Pendant Group Hydrolysis: Functional groups attached to the polymer chain may hydrolyze, altering the hydrogel’s properties. For example, ester-linked pendant groups in PEG-based hydrogels degrade in water. | 側基水解：連接到聚合物鏈的功能基團可能水解，改變水凝膠的性能。例如，PEG基水凝膠中的酯鍵側基在水中降解。

Types of Degradation | 降解類型 :

  • Preprogrammed: Degradation is controlled by the hydrogel’s chemical composition (e.g., hydrolytically labile bonds). | 預程式：降解由水凝膠的化學組成控制（例如，水解不穩定鍵）。
  • Cell-Dictated: Cells secrete enzymes (e.g., collagenases) that degrade the hydrogel, useful in tissue engineering. | 細胞主導：細胞分泌酶（如膠原酶）降解水凝膠，適用於組織工程。
  • Externally Triggered: Stimuli like pH, temperature, or light trigger degradation. For example, pH-sensitive hydrogels degrade in acidic tumor microenvironments. | 外部觸發：pH、溫度或光等刺激觸發降解。例如，pH敏感水凝膠在酸性腫瘤微環境中降解。

Example | 示例 :
Poly(lactic-co-glycolic acid) (PLGA) hydrogels degrade over weeks to months, releasing encapsulated drugs and eventually clearing from the body. | 聚(乳酸-共-乙醇酸)（PLGA）水凝膠在數週至數月內降解，釋放包封的藥物並最終從體內清除。

Key Insight | 關鍵見解 :
Controlled degradation allows hydrogels to serve temporary roles, such as delivering drugs or supporting tissue regeneration, before safely breaking down. | 控制降解使水凝膠能夠在藥物傳遞或組織再生等臨時角色中發揮作用，然後安全分解。

# Practice Questions | 練習題

❓Test Questions on Polymers and Hydrogels | 聚合物與水凝膠測驗問題

Q1. What does a polymer like Polycaprolactone behave under Tg (−60 °C) and above Tm (60 °C)? | 聚己內酯等聚合物在Tg (−60°C)以下和Tm (60°C)以上的行為如何？
a. Glass; gel | 玻璃態；凝膠
b. Rubber; rubber | 橡膠態；橡膠態
c. Glass; rubber | 玻璃態；橡膠態
d. Glass; liquid | 玻璃態；液態

✅ Answer: d. Glass; liquid | 玻璃態；液態
Explanation | 解析 :
Below Tg, the polymer is in a glassy, rigid state. Above Tm, crystalline regions melt, and the polymer becomes a viscous liquid. | 在玻璃轉化溫度以下，聚合物呈現玻璃態，剛性強。在熔點以上，晶體區域熔化，聚合物變為粘性液體。

Q2. Why was PVC phased out from water bottles and hospital usage? | 為何PVC從水瓶和醫院使用中被逐步淘汰？
a. High price | 價格高
b. Insufficient performance | 性能不足
c. Cannot degrade after disposal | 丟棄後無法降解
d. Toxic plasticizers (e.g., BPA, phthalates) | 有毒增塑劑（例如，BPA、鄰苯二甲酸酯）

✅ Answer: d. Toxic plasticizers | 有毒增塑劑
Explanation | 解析 :
PVC requires plasticizers like phthalates, which are toxic and can leach into fluids, raising health concerns. | PVC需添加鄰苯二甲酸酯等增塑劑來變柔軟，這些物質具有毒性，可能滲入液體中，對健康有害。

Q3. What forces contribute to hydrogel formation in PNIPAM above LCST? | PNIPAM在LCST以上形成水凝膠的力是什麼？
a. Ionic bond | 離子鍵
b. Metallic bond | 金屬鍵
c. Covalent bond | 共價鍵
d. Van der Waals force | 范德華力

✅ Answer: d. Van der Waals force | 范德華力
Explanation | 解析 :
PNIPAM forms physical hydrogels via hydrophobic interactions and Van der Waals forces above LCST. | PNIPAM在超過LCST時，因疏水作用與范德華力形成物理水凝膠，非共價鍵。

Q4. Why are hydrogels used in tissue engineering? | 為何水凝膠用於組織工程？
a. Biocompatibility | 生物相容性
b. Biodegradability | 可生物降解性
c. Ease of chemical modification | 易於化學修飾
d. Soft-tissue-like structure/properties | 類似軟組織的結構/性能

✅ Answer: All of the above (a, b, c, d) | 以上皆是（a、b、c、d）
Explanation | 解析 :
Hydrogels mimic the extracellular matrix, are biocompatible, biodegradable, and tunable. | 水凝膠模仿細胞外基質，具備良好生物相容性、可降解性，且易於化學修飾，適合軟組織應用。

Q5. What is the ideal mesh size for hydrogels used in cell encapsulation (cell size ~10 μm)? | 用於細胞包封的水凝膠理想網孔大小（細胞大小約10微米）是多少？
a. >10 μm | >10微米
b. <10 μm | <10微米
c. <10 μm and >0.01 nm | <10微米且>0.01奈米
d. None of the above | 以上皆非

✅ Answer: c. <10 μm and >0.01 nm | <10微米且>0.01奈米
Explanation | 解析 :
Mesh size must be smaller than cells to retain them, but large enough for nutrient diffusion. | 網孔大小需小於細胞以包覆，但又要足夠大以利養分與廢物擴散。

Q6. What are potential ways to change mesh size of hydrogels? | 改變水凝膠網孔大小的可能方法是什麼？
a. Density of linkers between polymer chains | 聚合物鏈之間連結密度
b. Concentration of polymers | 聚合物濃度
c. pH of the swelling media | 膨脹介質的pH值
d. All of the above | 以上皆是

✅ Answer: d. All of the above | 以上皆是
Explanation | 解析 :
All listed factors influence crosslinking density and swelling, thus affecting mesh size. | 所有列出的因素都會影響交聯密度和膨脹，從而影響網孔大小。

Q7. Why did nanoparticles affect the glass transition temperature (Tg) of PMMA differently in two studies? | 為何奈米粒子在兩項研究中對PMMA的玻璃轉化溫度（Tg）影響不同？
Ash et al. (2004): Alumina nanoparticles ↓ Tg | Ash等人（2004）：氧化鋁奈米粒子降低Tg
Agrawal et al. (2011): ZnS nanoparticles ↑ Tg | Agrawal等人（2011）：ZnS奈米粒子提高Tg

✅ Answer:
The effect of nanoparticles on Tg depends on how they interact with the polymer chains:
Alumina nanoparticles may disrupt chain packing, increasing free volume and lowering Tg.
ZnS nanoparticles may restrict chain mobility due to stronger interfacial interactions, raising Tg. |
奈米粒子對Tg的影響取決於其與聚合物鏈的相互作用：
氧化鋁奈米粒子可能破壞鏈的排列，增加自由體積，導致Tg下降。
ZnS奈米粒子可能限制鏈的運動性，使Tg上升。

Explanation | 解析 :
The effect of nanoparticles on Tg depends on their interaction with the polymer matrix. Alumina nanoparticles may act as spacers, reducing chain interactions and increasing free volume, thus lowering Tg. Conversely, ZnS nanoparticles may form strong interfacial bonds, restricting chain mobility and increasing Tg. | 奈米粒子對Tg的影響取決於其與聚合物基質的相互作用。氧化鋁奈米粒子可能作為間隔物，減少鏈間相互作用並增加自由體積，從而降低Tg。相反，ZnS奈米粒子可能形成強界面鍵，限制鏈的移動性，從而提高Tg。

Q8. Propose a synthesis method and list 3 unique properties of PCL (Polycaprolactone). | 提出PCL（聚己內酯）的合成方法並列出三個獨特性能。

✅ Answer:
Synthesis: Ring-opening polymerization of ε-caprolactone using a catalyst like stannous octoate.
Three unique properties of PCL:

  • Biodegradable – slowly degrades in physiological conditions.
  • Thermoplastic – easy to process and mold.
  • Good mechanical strength – suitable for load-bearing applications like bone scaffolds. |
    合成方法：使用辛酸亞錫等催化劑進行ε-己內酯的開環聚合。
    PCL的三個獨特性能：
  • 可生物降解 – 在生理條件下緩慢降解。
  • 熱塑性 – 易於加工和成型。
  • 良好機械強度 – 適用於骨支架等承重應用。

Explanation | 解析 :
Ring-opening polymerization is a common method for synthesizing PCL, allowing precise control over molecular weight. Its biodegradability, thermoplasticity, and mechanical strength make it ideal for biomedical applications like scaffolds and sutures. | 開環聚合是合成PCL的常用方法，可精確控制分子量。其可生物降解性、熱塑性和機械強度使其成為生物醫學應用的理想選擇，如支架和縫線。

Q9. Label the stress-strain curve of a 3D-printed PCL scaffold. | 標記3D列印PCL支架的應力-應變曲線。

Identify:

  • Elastic region
  • Yield stress
  • Plastic region
  • Ultimate tensile strength
  • Fracture point
    識別：
  • 彈性區
  • 屈服應力
  • 塑性區
  • 極限抗拉強度
  • 斷裂點

✅ Answer:

  • Elastic region: Initial linear portion of the curve (reversible deformation).
  • Yield stress: Point where plastic (permanent) deformation begins.
  • Plastic region: Nonlinear region after yield, material stretches without much increase in stress.
  • Ultimate tensile strength (UTS): Maximum stress the material can withstand.
  • Fracture point: Where the material breaks. |
  • 彈性區：曲線初期線性部分（可逆變形）。
  • 屈服應力：開始產生永久變形的點。
  • 塑性區：屈服後的非線性區域，材料延展但應力變化不大。
  • 極限抗拉強度（UTS）：材料可承受的最大應力。
  • 斷裂點：材料最終破裂的位置。

Explanation | 解析 :
The stress-strain curve of a 3D-printed PCL scaffold shows its mechanical behavior under tensile load. The elastic region indicates reversible deformation, followed by yielding where permanent deformation starts. The plastic region shows ductility, and the UTS marks the peak strength before fracture. | 3D列印PCL支架的應力-應變曲線顯示其在拉伸負載下的機械行為。彈性區表示可逆變形，隨後是屈服點，開始永久變形。塑性區顯示延展性，極限抗拉強度標示峰值強度，隨後斷裂。

Q10. What are the degradation mechanisms of GelMA hydrogel? | GelMA水凝膠的降解機制是什麼？

✅ Answer:
GelMA (gelatin methacrylate) degrades via:

  • Enzymatic degradation: Enzymes like collagenase cleave peptide bonds.
  • Hydrolysis: Water breaks down ester or amide bonds in the polymer network. |
    GelMA（明膠甲基丙烯酸酯）通過以下方式降解：
  • 酶解作用：如膠原酶切斷肽鍵。
  • 水解反應：水分子分解聚合物網絡中的酯鍵或醯胺鍵。

Explanation | 解析 :
GelMA’s gelatin backbone is susceptible to enzymatic cleavage by collagenases, while its methacryloyl groups introduce hydrolyzable ester bonds, enabling degradation in physiological environments. | GelMA的明膠主鏈易受膠原酶的酶解作用，而其甲基丙烯醯基團引入可水解的酯鍵，使其在生理環境中降解。

Q11. What is the Simon IVC filter made of? | Simon下腔靜脈濾器由什麼材料製成？

✅ Answer:
The Simon IVC filter is made of a shape memory metal, typically nitinol (nickel-titanium alloy). |
Simon下腔靜脈濾器由形狀記憶金屬製成，通常為鎳鈦合金。
Explanation | 解析 :
Nitinol provides flexibility, biocompatibility, and self-expanding properties, making it ideal for vascular implants like IVC filters. | 鎳鈦合金具有柔韌性、生物相容性和自擴展性，適合血管植入物，如下腔靜脈濾器。
