🧠 Nanomaterials: Origins & Impact | 奈米材料：起源與影響

Historical Foundations | 歷史基礎

  • The concept of nanotechnology was popularized by Richard Feynman in his 1959 lecture “There’s Plenty of Room at the Bottom” | 奈米技術的概念由理查德·費曼在1959年的演講“底部有足夠的空間”中推廣。
  • Feynman used vivid storytelling to advocate for atomic-scale engineering, inspiring decades of research | 費曼以生動的敘事方式提倡原子尺度工程，激發了數十年的研究。
  • Many of his predictions have since been realized through modern nanotechnology | 他的許多預測已通過現代奈米技術實現。

Evolution of Medical Applications | 醫療應用的演變

  • Initial breakthroughs (1980s–1990s): | 初期突破（1980年代至1990年代）：
    • PEGylation for drug stability | PEG化增強藥物穩定性
    • Targeting ribosomes | 靶向核糖體
    • Endometriosis treatments | 子宮內膜異位症治療
  • Current technologies: | 當前技術：
    • Lipid and ceramic nanoparticles | 脂質與陶瓷奈米粒子
    • Polymer micelles and protein carriers | 聚合物膠束與蛋白質載體
    • Cell-based and viral delivery systems | 基於細胞與病毒的傳遞系統

🔬 Key Properties of Nanomaterials | 奈米材料的關鍵特性

Surface-to-Volume Ratio | 表面積與體積比

  • As particle size decreases, the proportion of surface atoms increases: | 隨著粒徑減小，表面原子的比例增加：
    • ~90% for single-atom layers | 單原子層約90%
    • ~35% for 7-layer clusters | 7層簇約35%
    • <5% for 30 nm particles | 30奈米粒子小於5%
  • This enhances chemical reactivity and biological interaction | 這增強了化學反應性和生物交互作用。

Size-Dependent Behavior | 尺寸依賴行為

  • Nanomaterials exhibit unique properties not seen in bulk materials | 奈米材料展現出與大塊材料不同的獨特特性。
  • Operate in a transitional zone between molecular and macroscopic scales | 在分子與宏觀尺度之間的過渡區運作。
  • Enable novel applications in medicine, electronics, and energy | 實現醫學、電子與能源的新應用。

🟡 Gold Nanoparticles & Localized Surface Plasmon Resonance (LSPR) | 金奈米粒子與局部表面電漿共振

Discovery & Physical Properties | 發現與物理特性

  • First observed by Michael Faraday in 1857: wine-colored gold colloids | 1857年由邁克爾·法拉第首次觀察：酒紅色金膠體。
  • Gold nanoparticles have a lower melting point than bulk gold | 金奈米粒子的熔點低於大塊金。
  • Exhibit Localized Surface Plasmon Resonance (LSPR): | 展現局部表面電漿共振（LSPR）：
    • Oscillation of conduction electrons in response to light | 傳導電子響應光的振盪
    • Responsible for vivid colors (e.g., red from 520 nm green light absorption) | 負責鮮艷的顏色（例如，520奈米綠光吸收產生紅色）

Applications | 應用

  • Biosensors: | 生物感測器： Fluorescence quenching for detection | 螢光淬滅用於檢測
  • mRNA detection: | mRNA檢測： Using detachable linkers | 使用可分離連接物
  • Protein biomarkers: | 蛋白質生物標誌物： Enhanced sensitivity via plasmonic effects | 通過電漿效應增強靈敏度

Tuning LSPR | 調節LSPR

  • Size Increase | 尺寸增加 → Red shift in absorption | 吸收紅移
  • Shape Control | 形狀控制 (e.g., nanorods) → Longitudinal resonance in far-red region | （例如奈米棒）→遠紅區的縱向共振
  • Aggregation | 聚集 → Red shift due to interparticle coupling | 因粒子間耦合導致紅移

🌈 Quantum Dots (QDs): Color by Size | 量子點：尺寸決定顏色

Overview | 概述

  • Semiconductor nanocrystals with size-dependent emission | 具有尺寸依賴發射的半導體奈米晶體
  • Recognized by the 2022 Nobel Prize | 2022年諾貝爾獎認可

Mechanism | 機制

  • Smaller QDs → Larger band gap → Emission at shorter wavelengths (blue) | 較小的量子點→較大的能隙→較短波長發射（藍色）
  • Larger QDs → Smaller band gap → Emission at longer wavelengths (red) | 較大的量子點→較小的能隙→較長波長發射（紅色）

Manufacturing | 製造

  • Synthesized at ~300°C using cadmium, selenium, and sulfur | 在約300°C下使用鎘、硒和硫合成
  • Stabilized with ZnS shells and organic ligands | 使用ZnS殼和有機配體穩定

Advantages | 優勢

  • Photostability: Resistant to photobleaching | 光穩定性：抗光漂白
  • Broad absorption with narrow emission | 寬吸收與窄發射
  • Used in QLED displays, solar cells, and bioimaging | 用於QLED顯示器、太陽能電池和生物成像

🧪 Liposomes & Lipid Nanoparticles (LNPs) | 脂質體與脂質奈米粒子

Liposomes | 脂質體

  • Discovered by Alec Bangham in the 1960s | 1960年代由Alec Bangham發現
  • Composed of a double lipid bilayer enclosing an aqueous core | 由雙層脂質膜包圍水性核心組成
  • Can encapsulate both hydrophilic and hydrophobic drugs | 可包封親水性和疏水性藥物
  • Common components: phospholipids, PEG-lipids, cholesterol | 常見成分：磷脂、PEG-脂質、膽固醇
  • Fabrication: freeze-drying → hydration → size control | 製造：冷凍乾燥→水合→尺寸控制

Lipid Nanoparticles (LNPs) | 脂質奈米粒子

  • Solid lipid-based particles for nucleic acid delivery | 用於核酸傳遞的固體脂質粒子
  • Fabricated via microfluidics | 通過微流控技術製造
  • Typical formulation: 12 mg/mL lipids, 25% ethanol | 典型配方：12毫克/毫升脂質，25%乙醇
  • Purification: tangential flow filtration (TFF), dialysis, size exclusion | 純化：切向流過濾（TFF）、透析、尺寸排阻

🧭 Nanomaterial Fabrication Strategies | 奈米材料製造策略

Bottom-Up Approaches | 自下而上方法

  • Chemical synthesis: e.g., gold nanoparticles via citrate reduction | 化學合成：例如，通過檸檬酸還原製備金奈米粒子
  • Self-assembly: lipid vesicles, micelles | 自組裝：脂質囊泡、膠束
  • Spray drying: for drug particle formation | 噴霧乾燥：用於藥物粒子形成

Top-Down Approaches | 自上而下方法

  • PRINT® technology: Particle Replication in Non-wetting Templates | PRINT®技術：非潤濕模板中的粒子複製
  • Photolithography: Inspired by microelectronics | 光刻技術：受微電子啟發
  • Graphene exfoliation: Electrochemical peeling | 石墨烯剝離：電化學剝離

Surface Stabilization | 表面穩定

  • Charge-based (e.g., citrate): unstable in salt solutions | 基於電荷（例如檸檬酸）：在鹽溶液中不穩定
  • Steric-based (e.g., PEG): preferred for stability and protein corona prevention | 基於空間位阻（例如PEG）：穩定性和防止蛋白冠層的首選

💊 Nanomedicine: Benefits & Challenges | 奈米醫學：益處與挑戰

Benefits of Nanoparticle-Based Drug Delivery | 基於奈米粒子的藥物傳遞益處

  • Improved bioavailability and solubility | 改善生物利用度和溶解度
  • Suitable for intravenous (IV) administration | 適合靜脈（IV）給藥
  • Extended circulation time in the body | 延長體內循環時間
  • Enhanced tissue penetration | 增強組織滲透
  • Targeted accumulation at disease sites | 靶向疾病部位積累

Design Considerations | 設計考量

  • Size: | 尺寸：
    • 150 nm → accumulation in liver, lungs, spleen | 150奈米→在肝臟、肺部、脾臟積累
    • <10 nm → rapid clearance via kidneys | 小於10奈米→經腎臟快速清除
  • Shape: | 形狀：
    • Disc-shaped particles → liver/lung/spleen targeting | 盤狀粒子→靶向肝臟/肺部/脾臟
  • Surface Charge: | 表面電荷：
    • Positive charge → increased uptake in liver/lung | 正電荷→肝臟/肺部攝取增加

Delivery Challenges | 傳遞挑戰

  • Macrophage uptake reduces circulation time | 巨噬細胞攝取減少循環時間
  • Non-specific organ distribution | 非特異性器官分佈
  • Laminar blood flow limits vascular wall interaction | 層流限制血管壁交互
  • Tumor pressure hinders deep tissue penetration | 腫瘤壓力阻礙深層組織滲透

PRINT® Technology Applications | PRINT®技術應用

  • Used by Liquidia for lung-targeted delivery | Liquidia用於肺部靶向傳遞
  • Particle size (1–5 μm) affects deposition depth | 粒子尺寸（1-5微米）影響沉積深度
  • Particle shape influences biodistribution | 粒子形狀影響生物分佈

⚡ Charge-Stabilized Nanoparticles | 電荷穩定奈米粒子

  • Less stable than PEG-coated particles | 比PEG塗層粒子穩定性低
  • Stabilization via electric double layer: | 通過電雙層穩定：
    • Stern layer: tightly bound ions | 斯特恩層：緊密結合的離子
    • Diffusive layer: loosely bound ions | 擴散層：鬆散結合的離子
  • Salt exposure disrupts the double layer → aggregation | 鹽暴露破壞電雙層→聚集

🧬 Gene Delivery Using Nanoparticles | 使用奈米粒子進行基因傳遞

Central Dogma of Molecular Biology | 分子生物學中心法則

  • DNA → mRNA → Protein | DNA → mRNA → 蛋白質

Types of Therapeutic Nucleic Acids | 治療性核酸類型

  • DNA: | DNA： e.g., Luxturna (FDA-approved in 2017) | 例如Luxturna（2017年FDA批准）
  • mRNA: | mRNA： e.g., Pfizer/Moderna COVID-19 vaccines | 例如輝瑞/莫德納COVID-19疫苗
  • siRNA: | siRNA： e.g., Patisiran (FDA-approved in 2018) | 例如Patisiran（2018年FDA批准）
  • microRNA: | 微RNA： experimental, not FDA-approved | 實驗性，未經FDA批准

Delivery Methods | 傳遞方法

  • Viral Vectors: | 病毒載體：
    • Retrovirus, Adenovirus, AAV, HSV | 逆轉錄病毒、腺病毒、AAV、HSV
  • Non-Viral Methods: | 非病毒方法：
    • Gene gun, Electroporation, Nanoparticles | 基因槍、電穿孔、奈米粒子

Role of Lipid Nanoparticles (LNPs) | 脂質奈米粒子（LNPs）的角色

  • Protect nucleic acids from nucleases | 保護核酸免受核酸酶降解
  • Avoid renal clearance | 避免腎臟清除
  • Facilitate endocytosis | 促進內吞作用
  • Proposed structures: onion-like, cake-like, solid core | 提出的結構：洋蔥狀、蛋糕狀、固體核心

  • Viral vectors still dominate gene delivery | 病毒載體仍主導基因傳遞

Tutorial-5 | 教程-5

Q1. Which is correct in terms of surface plasmon resonance (SPR)? | 關於表面電漿共振（SPR），哪個是正確的？

A. SPR involves valence band electrons | SPR涉及價帶電子
B. SPR involves conduction band electrons | SPR涉及傳導帶電子
C. SPR involves magnetic field oscillation | SPR涉及磁場振盪
D. None of the above | 以上皆非

✅ B. SPR involves conduction band electrons | SPR涉及傳導帶電子
Explanation: SPR is caused by the collective oscillation of conduction electrons in resonance with the electric field of incident light | 解釋：SPR由傳導電子的集體振盪引起，與入射光的電場共振。

Q2. What is the difference between LSPR and SPR? | LSPR與SPR的區別是什麼？

A. Substrate | 基板
B. Plasmon | 電漿
C. Signal | 信號
D. All | 全部

✅ D. All | 全部

Explanation: SPR occurs on flat metal surfaces; LSPR occurs on nanoparticles. Differences include substrate type, plasmon behavior, and signal detection | 解釋：SPR發生在平坦金屬表面；LSPR發生在奈米粒子上。差異包括基板類型、電漿行為和信號檢測。

Q3. Compare the longest SPR wavelength among gold nanostructures: | 比較金奈米結構中最長的SPR波長：

  1. 10 nm AuNPs | 10奈米金奈米粒子
  2. 50 nm AuNPs | 50奈米金奈米粒子
  3. 10×60 nm nanorods | 10×60奈米奈米棒
  4. 10×40 nm nanorods | 10×40奈米奈米棒

A. 1 > 3 > 2 > 4
B. 2 > 1 > 3 > 4
C. 3 > 4 > 2 > 1
D. 4 > 3 > 2 > 1

✅ C. 3 > 4 > 2 > 1

Explanation: Nanorods with higher aspect ratios show longer SPR wavelengths. Size also contributes, but shape dominates | 解釋：高縱橫比的奈米棒顯示較長的SPR波長。尺寸也有影響，但形狀主導。

Q4. Which nanoparticles change color when 3 M NaCl is added? | 添加3M NaCl時，哪些奈米粒子會改變顏色？

A. Citrate-stabilized 20 nm AuNPs | 檸檬酸穩定的20奈米金奈米粒子
B. PEG-stabilized AuNPs | PEG穩定的金奈米粒子
C. CdSe QDs with PEG | 帶PEG的CdSe量子點
D. Lipid nanoparticles with PEG | 帶PEG的脂質奈米粒子

✅ A. Citrate-stabilized 20 nm AuNPs | 檸檬酸穩定的20奈米金奈米粒子

Explanation: Salt disrupts electrostatic stabilization in citrate-coated AuNPs, causing aggregation and color change | 解釋：鹽破壞檸檬酸塗層金奈米粒子的靜電穩定性，導致聚集和顏色變化。

Q5. Quantum dots emitting blue, green, and red light—compare retention time in size exclusion chromatography: | 發射藍光、綠光和紅光的量子點—比較尺寸排阻層析中的保留時間：

A. Blue > Green > Red
B. Green > Red > Blue
C. Red > Green > Blue
D. Red > Blue > Green

✅ A. Blue > Green > Red

Explanation: Smaller QDs (blue) elute slower due to more pore interaction. Larger QDs (red) elute faster | 解釋：較小的量子點（藍色）由於更多孔隙交互作用而洗脫較慢。較大的量子點（紅色）洗脫較快。

Q6. What method can be used to make liposomes? | 哪種方法可用於製造脂質體？

A. Electrochemical exfoliation | 電化學剝離
B. PRINT technology | PRINT技術
C. Spray drying | 噴霧乾燥
D. Self-assembly | 自組裝

✅ D. Self-assembly | 自組裝

Explanation: Liposomes form spontaneously when amphiphilic lipids self-assemble in water | 解釋：當兩親性脂質在水中自組裝時，脂質體會自發形成。

Q7. Major difference between liposomes and lipid nanoparticles? | 脂質體與脂質奈米粒子的主要區別？

A. Size | 尺寸
B. Surface functional group | 表面官能團
C. Composition and function | 組成與功能
D. Surface charge | 表面電荷

✅ C. Composition and function | 組成與功能

Explanation: Liposomes have bilayer structures; LNPs are solid or amorphous. Their composition and delivery roles differ | 解釋：脂質體具有雙層結構；脂質奈米粒子為固體或非晶態。它們的組成和傳遞角色不同。

Q8. Which factor influences nanoparticle biodistribution in vivo? | 哪些因素影響奈米粒子在體內的生物分佈？

A. Size | 尺寸
B. Surface charge | 表面電荷
C. Shape | 形狀
D. All of the above | 以上全部

✅ D. All of the above | 以上全部

Explanation: All these factors affect circulation, organ targeting, and cellular uptake | 解釋：所有這些因素都影響循環、器官靶向和細胞攝取。

Q9. Why are lipid nanoparticles needed for nucleic acid drug delivery? | 為什麼核酸藥物傳遞需要脂質奈米粒子？

A. In vivo degradation by nucleases | 體內核酸酶降解
B. Renal clearance | 腎臟清除
C. Negative charge prevents membrane crossing | 負電荷阻止膜穿越
D. All of the above | 以上全部

✅ D. All of the above | 以上全部

Explanation: LNPs protect nucleic acids, improve delivery efficiency, and prevent rapid clearance | 解釋：LNPs保護核酸，提高傳遞效率，並防止快速清除。

Q10. mRNA-1273 (Moderna COVID vaccine) is injected intramuscularly. | mRNA-1273（莫德納COVID疫苗）通過肌肉注射給藥。

  • Role of mRNA: Provides genetic instructions to antigen-presenting cells (APCs) to produce the spike (S) protein antigen | mRNA的角色：為抗原呈遞細胞（APCs）提供遺傳指令以產生刺突（S）蛋白抗原。
  • Role of lipid nanoparticles (LNPs): Protect mRNA from degradation, facilitate cellular uptake, and help deliver mRNA into APCs | 脂質奈米粒子（LNPs）的角色：保護mRNA免受降解，促進細胞攝取，並幫助將mRNA傳遞到APCs。
  • Target cells: APCs such as dendritic cells and Langerhans cells translate the mRNA into spike protein, initiating the immune response | 目標細胞：樹突細胞和朗格漢斯細胞等APCs將mRNA翻譯成刺突蛋白，啟動免疫反應。
