Surface Properties Characteristics | 表面性質特徵

  • Key Points | 要點:
    1. Surfaces have unique reactivity. | 表面具有獨特的反應性。
    2. The surface is inevitably different from the bulk. | 表面與體相不可避免地不同。
    3. Surface molecules can exhibit considerable mobility. | 表面分子可展現顯著的流動性。

Surface Dynamics | 表面動態

  • Observation: The movement of atoms and molecules near the surface in response to the external environment is highly significant. | 觀察：表面附近的原子和分子對外部環境的響應具有重要意義。

Protein-Surface Interaction | 蛋白質-表面交互作用

  • Effect of Protein Size: Larger proteins with more amino acids can make more interactions with a surface. | 蛋白質大小的影響：具有更多氨基酸的較大蛋白質可與表面進行更多交互作用。

Surface Characteristics | 表面特性

  • Factors | 因素:
    • Roughness (A) | 粗糙度 (A)
    • Patterns (C) | 圖案 (C)
    • Wettability, surface mobility, chemical composition (B) | 潤濕性、表面流動性、化學組成 (B)
    • Electrical charge, crystallinity (E) | 電荷、結晶度 (E)
    • Heterogeneity (D & F) | 異質性 (D & F)

Cosmetic Contact Lenses | 彩色隱形眼鏡

  • Context: Pigments in cosmetic contact lenses may alter surface properties, impacting comfort compared to clear lenses. | 背景：彩色隱形眼鏡中的色素可能改變表面性質，影響與透明隱形眼鏡相比的舒適度。
  • Factors | 因素:
    • Location and depth of pigment particles from the lens surface | 色素顆粒與鏡片表面的位置和深度
    • Coefficient of friction | 摩擦係數
    • Surface roughness | 表面粗糙度
  • Source: Johnson & Johnson Vision Inc, Eye Contact Lens, 2018 Nov; 44(2):S256

Surface Analysis Considerations | 表面分析注意事項

  • Challenges | 挑戰:
    1. All methods can alter the surface. | 所有方法都可能改變表面。
    2. Potential for artifacts requires corroborative information from multiple methods to construct a complete surface profile. | 可能出現的偽影需要多種方法提供佐證信息以構建完整的表面輪廓。

  • Purpose: Directly measures surface wettability; indirectly probes surface energy, roughness, heterogeneity, contamination, and molecular mobility. | 目的：直接測量表面潤濕性；間接探測表面能量、粗糙度、異質性、污染和分子流動性。

Scanning Electron Microscopy (SEM) | 掃描電子顯微鏡（SEM）

  • Function: Uses a high-energy electron beam (5-100 keV) rastered on a specimen under vacuum. | 功能：使用高能電子束（5-100 keV）在真空下對樣品進行掃描。
  • Mechanism: Low-energy secondary electrons (1-20 eV) are emitted from impact points, with intensity depending on atomic composition and geometry. | 機制：從撞擊點發射低能二次電子（1-20 eV），強度取決於原子組成和幾何形狀。
  • Note: Insulating materials require metallic coating; images reflect the coating surface, not the underlying material. | 注意：絕緣材料需要金屬塗層；圖像反映塗層表面，而非底層材料。

SEM Imaging | 掃描電子顯微鏡成像

  • Electrons Used: Secondary and backscattered electrons are used for SEM imaging. | 使用的電子：二次電子和背散射電子用於SEM成像。

Electron-Solid Interactions | 電子-固體交互作用

  • Regions | 區域:
    • Secondary Electron (SE): Produced wherever primary electrons go, but only those near the surface reach the SE detector for imaging. | 二次電子（SE）：只要初級電子到達就會產生，但只有靠近表面的二次電子到達SE檢測器進行成像。
    • Backscattered Electron (BSE): Produced with higher energy, travels farther than SE, provides better Z-contrast. | 背散射電子（BSE）：以較高能量產生，傳播距離比SE遠，提供更好的Z對比。
    • X-ray Region: Used for Energy-Dispersive X-ray Spectroscopy (EDX), travels farther than BSE, best for analytical purposes. | X射線區域：用於能量分散X射線光譜（EDX），傳播距離比BSE遠，最適合分析用途。
  • Applications | 應用:
    • SE: Best spatial resolution | 二次電子：最佳空間分辨率
    • BSE: Better Z-contrast | 背散射電子：更好的Z對比
    • EDX: Best for elemental analysis | 能量分散X射線光譜：最佳元素分析

X-ray Photoelectron Spectroscopy (XPS/ESCA) | X射線光電子能譜（XPS/ESCA）

  • Mechanism: An X-ray photon ejects an electron from an atom’s inner shell (e.g., K shell). The kinetic energy of the ejected electron allows calculation of binding energy, identifying elements. | 機制：X射線光子從原子內殼（例如K殼）中逐出電子。逐出電子的動能可用於計算結合能，識別元素。
  • Applications | 應用:
    • Identifies all elements except H and He (concentrations >0.1%). | 識別除H和He外的所有元素（濃度>0.1%）。
    • Semi-quantitative elemental surface composition (±10%). | 半定量元素表面組成（±10%）。
    • Provides molecular environment details (oxidation state, bonding atoms). | 提供分子環境細節（氧化態、鍵結原子）。
    • Detects aromatic/unsaturated structures via shake-up transitions. | 通過激發躍遷檢測芳香族/不飽和結構。
    • Identifies organic groups using derivatization reactions. | 使用衍生反應識別有機基團。
    • Non-destructive elemental depth profiles (100 Å). | 非破壞性元素深度輪廓（100 Å）。
    • Destructive depth profiles (thousands of Å) using argon etching (for inorganics). | 使用氬蝕刻進行破壞性深度輪廓（數千Å，用於無機物）。
    • Lateral surface composition variations (spatial resolution 8-150 µm). | 橫向表面組成變化（空間分辨率8-150 µm）。
    • Fingerprinting materials using valence band spectra and bonding orbitals. | 使用價帶光譜和鍵結軌道進行材料指紋識別。
    • Studies on hydrated (frozen) surfaces. | 研究水合（冷凍）表面。

Time-of-Flight Secondary Ion Mass Spectrometry (TOF-SIMS) | 飛行時間二次離子質譜（TOF-SIMS）

  • Mechanism: Bombards surface with accelerated ions, ejecting secondary ions, radicals, excited states, and neutrals. Only secondary ions are measured. | 機制：用加速離子轟擊表面，逐出二次離子、自由基、激發態和中性粒子。僅測量二次離子。
  • Purpose: Analyzes elemental, isotopic, or molecular composition of the outermost 1-2 nm. | 目的：分析最外層1-2奈米的元素、同位素或分子組成。
  • Static SIMS: Uses low ion doses for analysis. | 靜態SIMS：使用低離子劑量進行分析。

When to Use EDX, ESCA, or SIMS | 何時使用EDX、ESCA或SIMS

  • EDX: For elemental composition of materials. | 用於材料的元素組成。
  • ESCA (XPS): For quantifying surface contamination, understanding electronic structure, or analyzing elemental/chemical states with good energy resolution. | 用於量化表面污染、理解電子結構或分析具有良好能量分辨率的元素/化學狀態。
  • SIMS: For high-sensitivity trace element detection, high-resolution surface imaging, or molecular composition analysis. | 用於高靈敏度痕量元素檢測、高分辨率表面成像或分子組成分析。

Raman Spectroscopy | 拉曼光譜

  • Definition: Measures the energy difference (Raman shift) between incident and scattered light. | 定義：測量入射光與散射光之間的能量差（拉曼位移）。
  • Spectrum: Vertical axis (intensity of scattered light), horizontal axis (wavenumber of Raman shift, cm⁻¹). | 光譜：縱軸（散射光強度），橫軸（拉曼位移波數，cm⁻¹）。
  • Example: Raman spectrum of tetrachloromethane (532 nm green laser): | 範例：四氯化碳的拉曼光譜（532奈米綠色激光）:
    • Stokes scattering: Lower wavenumber (longer wavelength), typically used for analysis. | 斯托克斯散射：較低波數（較長波長），通常用於分析。
    • Anti-Stokes scattering: Higher wavenumber (shorter wavelength), also usable. | 反斯托克斯散射：較高波數（較短波長），也可用。

Surface-Enhanced Raman Spectroscopy (SERS) | 表面增強拉曼光譜（SERS）

  • Technique: Uses nanostructured metal surfaces (gold or silver) to amplify Raman scattering signals via localized surface plasmons, enabling ultra-sensitive detection (down to single-molecule level). | 技術：使用奈米結構金屬表面（金或銀）通過局部表面電漿子放大拉曼散射信號，實現超靈敏檢測（低至單分子水平）。

Bulk Properties | 體相性質

  • Role: Dictate the ability to carry loads without undue deflection, premature failure, or degradation. | 角色：決定在不發生過度變形、過早失效或降解的情況下承載負荷的能力。
  • Properties | 性質:
    • Mechanical (stress, strain, moduli) | 機械（應力、應變、模量）
    • Thermal (response to heat) | 熱（對熱的響應）
    • Optical (response to light) | 光學（對光的響應）
  • Categories | 類別:
    • Intrinsic: Depend on composition (e.g., density, heat capacity, stiffness). | 內在：取決於組成（例如，密度、熱容量、剛度）。
    • Extrinsic: Depend on microstructure (e.g., yield strength, optical/magnetic properties). | 外在：取決於微觀結構（例如，屈服強度、光學/磁性質）。

Physical Properties | 物理性質

  • Examples | 範例:
    • Crystallinity | 結晶度
    • Thermal transition points (melting point ((T_m)), glass transition point ((T_g))) | 熱轉換點（熔點((T_m))、玻璃化轉變點((T_g))）
    • Chemical composition | 化學組成
  • Impact: Chemical composition affects bulk properties, surface chemistry, and other characteristics. | 影響：化學組成影響體相性質、表面化學和其他特性。

Mechanical Properties | 機械性質

  • Purpose: Estimate behavior under mechanical loads (tension, compression, torsion, bending, cyclic loading, impact). | 目的：估計在機械負載（拉伸、壓縮、扭轉、彎曲、循環負載、衝擊）下的行為。
  • Influence: Highly affected by physical and chemical characteristics. | 影響：高度受物理和化學特性的影響。

Tensile/Compressive Testing | 拉伸/壓縮測試

  • Method: Applies uniaxial loading (tensile, compressive, or shear) at controlled amplitude and rate. | 方法：以控制的幅度和速率施加單軸負載（拉伸、壓縮或剪切）。
  • Setup: One end of the specimen is attached to a movable platform. | 設置：樣品的一端附著在可移動平台上。

Compression Testing | 壓縮測試

  • Application: Common for biomaterials under compressive forces (e.g., orthopedic implants). | 應用：常用於承受壓縮力的生物材料（例如，骨科植入物）。
  • Specimen: Cylindrical, with length at least twice the diameter. | 樣品：圓柱形，長度至少為直徑的兩倍。
  • Calculation: Stress-strain calculated similarly to tensile tests, but values are negative. | 計算：應力-應變計算與拉伸測試類似，但值為負。

Practice Questions | 練習題

Q1. What does a “real-world” material’s surface have?
a. Water
b. Oxides
c. Organic molecules
d. All of the above

✅ Answer: d. All of the above
Explanation: Real-world surfaces are rarely pristine; they typically have adsorbed water, oxide layers, and organic contaminants from the environment.
解析： 真實材料表面通常含有水分、氧化層及有機污染物，並非完全潔淨。

Q2. If a liquid does not wet a solid surface, then its angle of contact is:
a. Acute angle
b. Obtuse angle
c. Right angle
d. More than one of the above
e. None of the above

✅ Answer: b. Obtuse angle
Explanation: Poor wetting corresponds to a large contact angle (>90°), which is obtuse. Good wetting occurs when the angle is small (<90°).
解析： 液體不潤濕固體表面時，接觸角大於90°，屬於鈍角；良好潤濕時接觸角小於90°。

Q3. Dancing of small pieces of camphor on the surface of water is due to:
a. Viscosity
b. Surface tension
c. Weight
d. Lifting force

✅ Answer: b. Surface tension
Explanation: Camphor alters local surface tension, creating gradients that cause movement (Marangoni effect).
解析： 樟腦改變水面局部表面張力，產生梯度，導致樟腦片移動，稱為馬蘭哥尼效應。

Q4. Which one is true for cosmetic contact lenses?
a. They alter eye color or appearance, some also correct vision
b. They are harmless for anyone to use
c. Contact lenses are considered consumables
d. Cosmetic lenses are one-size-fits-all

✅ Answer: a. They alter eye color or appearance, some also correct vision
Explanation: Cosmetic lenses primarily change appearance; some have corrective power. They are not universally safe and require proper fitting.
解析： 美瞳主要改變眼睛外觀，有些具矯正功能，但並非人人安全，需適當配戴。

Q5. Difference between EDX, ESCA and SIMS’s measurement?
a. Excitation source
b. Detection signal
c. Profiling depth
d. All of the above

✅ Answer: d. All of the above
Explanation: These techniques differ in excitation (X-ray, electron, ion), detection (photon, electron, ion), and depth sensitivity (nm to μm scale).
解析： EDX、ESCA、SIMS在激發源、檢測信號及深度解析度上均有差異。

Q6. Which factor keeps changing in tensile stress and compression stress?
a. Young’s modulus of materials
b. Cross-sectional area of materials
c. Surface chemistry of materials
d. None of the above

✅ Answer: b. Cross-sectional area of materials
Explanation: Under tension or compression, the cross-sectional area changes due to deformation, while Young’s modulus remains constant for elastic range.
解析： 拉伸或壓縮時，截面積隨變形改變，而楊氏模量在彈性範圍內保持不變。

Q7. Why is PCL a better choice than PLGA in making scaffolds for skull regeneration?
a. Glass transition temperature (−60 °C vs 40–60 °C)
b. Melting temperature (60 °C vs 240 °C)
c. Degradation time (2–3 years vs <6 months)
d. Mechanical properties (>200 MPa vs 20–30 MPa)

✅ Answer: All of the above (a, b, c, d)
Explanation: PCL has lower Tg and Tm, making it easier to process; it degrades slowly, providing long-term support, and has superior mechanical strength compared to PLGA.
解析： PCL玻璃轉化溫度和熔點低，易加工；降解時間長，能長期支撐；機械強度高於PLGA，適合顱骨再生。

Q8. How to evaluate antiviral coating (e.g., MAP-1)?

Effectiveness
Dosage/coverage
Half-life/sustainability
Duration under conditions (shower, sunlight, moisture)

✅ Answer:
Evaluation involves testing antimicrobial efficacy, required dosage, durability under environmental stress, and longevity before reapplication.
解析： 評估需檢測抗菌效能、劑量覆蓋率、耐久性及在環境條件下的持續時間。
