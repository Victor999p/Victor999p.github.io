Topic 1 – Introduction to Polymers 聚合物簡介

大圖景 (Big Picture)

Polymers 聚合物 are large molecules essential for tissue engineering scaffolds due to their versatility in mimicking ECM 細胞外基質.

思維導圖 (Mind map):
Polymers 聚合物 → Natural 天然 (Wood/Rubber/DNA) → Synthetic 合成 (PE/PS/PEG) → Biomedical Suitability 生物醫學適用性 (Strength/Biodegradability/Fabrication 強度/生物降解性/加工性) → Disadvantages 缺點 (Deformation/Toxicity/Degradation 變形/毒性/降解)

必須掌握嘅要點 (Must-Know Facts)

  • Polymers revolutionized chemistry with major everyday impact (Lord Todd quote).
  • ECM 細胞外基質 components like collagen 膠原蛋白, proteins 蛋白質, and GAGs 糖胺聚糖 are polymers.
  • Suited for biomedical apps 生物醫學應用: low/high strength, protein attachment 蛋白質黏附 resistance/encouragement.
  • Biodegradable 生物可降解 or permanent; fabricable into complex shapes.
  • Disadvantages: lower strength than metals/ceramics, deform over time.
  • May deteriorate during sterilization 滅菌 or degrade catastrophically in body.
  • Release toxic byproducts 有毒副產物 possible.
  • Natural examples: Wood 木材, Rubber 橡膠, Leather 皮革, Cotton 棉花, Wool 羊毛, Silk 絲綢, DNA, Proteins 蛋白質.
  • Synthetic examples: Polystyrene (PS) 聚苯乙烯, PET 聚對苯二甲酸乙二醇酯, PE 聚乙烯, PEG 聚乙二醇, Kevlar 凱夫拉.
  • Polymers in scaffolds mimic ECM for cell support.

Topic 2 – Polymerization Types 聚合類型

大圖景 (Big Picture)

Polymerization 聚合作用 forms chains via addition 加成聚合 (non-degradable) or step-growth 逐步聚合 (degradable via hydrolysis 水解).

思維導圖 (Mind map):
Polymerization 聚合作用 → Addition 加成聚合 (Radicals/Ions 自由基/離子 → No labile bonds 無不穩定鍵) → Step-Growth 逐步聚合 (Condensation 縮合 → Ester bonds 酯鍵 → Hydrolysis 水解) → Examples 例子 (PE vs PLA/PGA/PLGA)

必須掌握嘅要點 (Must-Know Facts)

  • Addition 加成聚合: Double-bond monomers 雙鍵單體 like ethylene 乙烯, vinyl chloride 氯乙烯, styrene 苯乙烯, MMA 甲基丙烯酸甲酯.
  • Via radicals 自由基 or ions 離子; no labile bonds, not degradable.
  • Example: Ethylene → Polyethylene (PE) 聚乙烯.
  • Step-growth 逐步聚合: Functional group reactions 官能團反應, condensation 縮合.
  • Well-known: PLA 聚乳酸, PGA 聚乙醇酸, PLGA 聚乳酸-乙醇酸共聚物.
  • Degrade via main chain ester hydrolysis 酯鍵水解.
  • PLA from lactic acid 乳酸 condensation.
  • PGA from glycolic acid 乙醇酸.
  • PLGA copolymers tunable.

關鍵公式/圖表 (Key Formulas / Diagrams)

對比表 (Comparison Table) - Addition vs Step-Growth Polymerization 加成 vs 逐步聚合

Addition Polymerization 加成聚合Step-Growth Polymerization 逐步聚合
Double bonds break 雙鍵斷裂Functional groups react 官能團反應
Non-degradable chains 鏈不可降解Degradable via hydrolysis 可通過水解降解
Examples: PE, PVC 例子: PE，PVCExamples: PLA, PGA 例子: PLA，PGA

常見錯誤同考官陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Examiner Traps)

  • 混淆加成聚合與逐步聚合 (Mixing addition and step-growth): 陷阱題將 PLA 分類為加成聚合（實際係逐步聚合）。
  • 忘記逐步聚合嘅副產物 (Forgetting condensation byproduct in step-growth): 有水生成。

Topic 3 – Polymer Structures and States 聚合物結構與狀態

大圖景 (Big Picture)

Polymers exist as crystalline 結晶, amorphous 無定形, or semi-crystalline 半結晶, affecting melting 熔化 and glass transitions 玻璃化轉變.

思維導圖 (Mind map):
Structures 結構 → Crystals 晶體 (Ordered/Lattice/Sharp Tm 有序/晶格/銳利Tm) → Amorphous 無定形 (No order/Tg 無序/Tg) → Polymers 聚合物 (Semi-crystalline/Folding/Stacking 半結晶/摺疊/堆疊) → States 狀態 (Rubber/Glass 橡膠態/玻璃態)

必須掌握嘅要點 (Must-Know Facts)

  • Crystals 晶體: Infinite 3D lattice 無限三維晶格, sharp Tm 熔點.
  • Amorphous 無定形: No structure, sharp Tm.
  • Small molecules 小分子: 100% crystalline or amorphous.
  • Polymer crystalline: Intramolecular folding/stacking 分子內摺疊/堆疊.
  • Semi-crystalline 半結晶: Never 100% crystalline, have Tm.
  • Rubber/Glass 橡膠態/玻璃態: Frozen liquid 凍結液體, no long-range order.
  • Tg 玻璃化轉變溫度: Below Tg, no cooperative rotation/translation 協同旋轉/平移.
  • Transition: Rubbery to glassy hard state.
  • Crystallization 結晶 favored by simple structures, small side groups.
  • Linear, non-crosslinked polymers crystallize better.
  • Block/alternating copolymers 嵌段/交替共聚物, slow cooling aid crystallization.
  • Crystalline: Stronger, resistant to dissolution.

對比表 (Comparison Table) - Crystalline vs Amorphous 結晶 vs 無定形

Crystalline 結晶Amorphous 無定形
Ordered lattice 有序晶格No order 無序
Sharp Tm 銳利熔點Tg only 只有玻璃化轉變溫度
Stronger 更強Flexible 更柔韌

常見錯誤同考官陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Examiner Traps)

  • 假設聚合物100%結晶 (Assuming polymers are 100% crystalline): 陷阱題 - 聚合物是半結晶的。
  • 混淆Tm同Tg (Confusing Tm and Tg)。

Topic 4 – Polymer Properties 聚合物特性

大圖景 (Big Picture)

Properties like crystallinity 結晶度, thermal behavior 熱行為, and side groups 側基 influence polymer performance in scaffolds.

思維導圖 (Mind map):
Properties 特性 → Crystallinity 結晶度 (Favored by simplicity 有利於簡單結構) → Side Groups 側基 (Flexible lower Tg 柔韌側基降低Tg) → Thermal 熱性能 (Tg/Tm values) → Effects 影響 (Free volume/Motion 自由體積/運動)

必須掌握嘅要點 (Must-Know Facts)

  • Crystallization 結晶: Simple/small side groups, linear, non-network.
  • Slow cooling for chain motion.
  • Crystalline stronger/resistant.
  • Flexible side groups: Tg decreases with length.
  • Side chains rotate, increase free volume 自由體積, lower Tg.
  • Thermal examples: Nylon 6,6 尼龍6,6 Tg 45°C Tm 267°C.
  • UHMWPE 超高分子量聚乙烯 Tg -125°C Tm 140°C.
  • Silicone 矽橡膠 Tg -123°C Tm -29°C.
  • PMMA 聚甲基丙烯酸甲酯 Tg 105°C Tm 160°C.
  • PDLLA 聚消旋乳酸 Tg 50°C amorphous.
  • PCL 聚己內酯 Tg -60°C Tm 57°C.
  • PGA 聚乙醇酸 Tg 35°C Tm 210°C.

關鍵公式/圖表 (Key Formulas / Diagrams)

對比表 (Comparison Table) - Short vs Long Side Groups 短 vs 長側基

Short Side Groups 短側基Long Side Groups 長側基
Higher Tg 較高TgLower Tg 較低Tg
Less free volume 較少自由體積More free volume 較多自由體積
Restricted motion 運動受限Easier rotation 易於旋轉

常見錯誤同考官陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Examiner Traps)

  • 認為長側基會提高Tg (Thinking longer sides increase Tg): 實際上會降低。
  • 忽略側基對堆積嘅影響 (Ignoring side chain effects on packing)。

Topic 5 – Crosslinked Networks and Hydrogels 交聯網絡與水凝膠

大圖景 (Big Picture)

Crosslinks 交聯鍵 create networks that swell in water to form hydrogels 水凝膠 for cell environments.

思維導圖 (Mind map):
Crosslinks 交聯鍵 (Covalent/H-bond/Entangle 共價鍵/氫鍵/纏結) → Networks 網絡 (Increased MW/Swell 分子量增加/溶脹) → Hydrogels 水凝膠 (Water-swollen/Balanced forces 水溶脹/平衡力) → Apps 應用 (Nutrients/Waste/Aqueous mimic 營養/廢物/水環境模擬)

必須掌握嘅要點 (Must-Know Facts)

  • Crosslinks: Covalent 共價鍵, H-bonding 氫鍵, entanglements 纏結.
  • Increase MW 分子量, swell in solvents (hydrogel).
  • Hydrogels 水凝膠: Water-swollen crosslinked structures.
  • From monomers or chain associations.
  • Why: Cells need aqueous for nutrients/waste.
  • Prepared by crosslinking then swelling.
  • PHEMA 聚羥基乙基甲基丙烯酸酯 common: Tissue-like water, degradation resistant.
  • Apps: Contacts 隱形眼鏡, wounds 傷口, kidney membranes 腎膜.
  • Swelling: Thermodynamic force 熱力學力 vs retractive 收縮力.
  • Highly swollen: Cellulose 纖維素, PVA 聚乙烯醇, PEG.
  • Moderately: PHEMA.

關鍵公式/圖表 (Key Formulas / Diagrams)

對比表 (Comparison Table) - Solid Porous Scaffolds vs Hydrogel Scaffolds 固體多孔支架 vs 水凝膠支架

Solid Porous Scaffolds 固體多孔支架Hydrogel Scaffolds 水凝膠支架
For bone (stable) 用於骨（穩定）For soft tissues (fill defects) 用於軟組織（填充缺損）
Degrade by hydrolysis 通過水解降解Incorporate cells/bioactives easily 易於包埋細胞/生物活性因子
Non-dissolving 不溶解Minimally invasive 微創

常見錯誤同考官陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Examiner Traps)

  • 混淆交聯與聚合 (Confusing crosslinks with polymerization): 陷阱題 - 水凝膠不需要交聯（實際需要）。
  • 忽略溶脹平衡 (Overlooking swelling balance)。

Topic 6 – Natural Polymer Scaffolds 天然聚合物支架

大圖景 (Big Picture)

Natural polymers from proteins 蛋白質/polysaccharides 多糖 provide biocompatible scaffolds but with variability risks.

思維導圖 (Mind map):
Natural Scaffolds 天然支架 → Proteins 蛋白質 (Collagen/Elastin 膠原蛋白/彈性蛋白) → Polysaccharides 多糖 (GAGs/Hyaluronan 糖胺聚糖/透明質酸) → Sources 來源 (Plants/Algae/Animals/Microbes 植物/藻類/動物/微生物) → Crosslinking 交聯 (Chemical/Physical 化學/物理)

必須掌握嘅要點 (Must-Know Facts)

  • Proteins: Amino acid polymers, include collagen, elastin, laminin.
  • DNA/RNA: Phosphate-ribose backbone, not for scaffolds.
  • Scaffolds: Proteins, polysaccharides, combos.
  • Sources: Collagen/fibrin/elastin (animals), soybean (plants), silk fibroin 絲素蛋白.
  • Gelatin 明膠: Partial hydrolysis of collagen from skin/bones.
  • Extraction: Reduce crosslinks, acid/water at temp.
  • Acidic pH speeds conversion.
  • Scaffolds: Crosslink via -OH/-NH2/-COOH/-SH.
  • Chemical mod tricky in water.
  • May alter solubility/conformation/activity 溶解性/構象/活性.
  • Collagen: Triple-helical 三螺旋, ECM main, immunogenicity risk 免疫原性風險.
  • Crosslink: Physical (moisture/heat/UV), chemical (glutaraldehyde 戊二醛).
  • Formaldehyde 甲醛: Fixes tissue by crosslinking aminos.
  • Polysaccharides: Monosaccharide polymers, glycosidic bonds 糖苷鍵.
  • GAGs: Unbranched 無分支, repeating disaccharides 重複雙糖單位 (chondroitin 硫酸軟骨素 etc.).

關鍵公式/圖表 (Key Formulas / Diagrams)

對比表 (Comparison Table) - Proteins vs Polysaccharides 蛋白質 vs 多糖

Proteins 蛋白質Polysaccharides 多糖
Amino acids 氨基酸Monosaccharides 單糖
Collagen/Gelatin 膠原蛋白/明膠GAGs/Hyaluronan 糖胺聚糖/透明質酸
Crosslink via groups 通過官能團交聯Glycosidic bonds 糖苷鍵

常見錯誤同考官陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Examiner Traps)

  • 認為DNA/RNA用作支架 (Thinking DNA/RNA used as scaffolds): 佢哋唔係。
  • 忽略天然聚合物嘅免疫原性 (Ignoring immunogenicity in natural polymers)。

Topic 7 – Specific Natural Polymers 特定天然聚合物

大圖景 (Big Picture)

Specific naturals like hyaluronan 透明質酸, matrigel 基質膠, fibrin 纖維蛋白, alginate 藻酸鹽, chitosan 殼聚糖 offer unique TE properties.

思維導圖 (Mind map):
Specifics 特定聚合物 → Hyaluronan 透明質酸 (Repair/Cartilage/Hydrophilic 修復/軟骨/親水) → Matrigel 基質膠 (ECM mix/Stem cells ECM混合物/幹細胞) → Fibrin 纖維蛋白 (Coagulation/Biocompatible 凝血/生物相容) → Alginate 藻酸鹽 (Algae/Ca crosslink 藻類/鈣交聯) → Chitosan 殼聚糖 (Arthropod/Cartilage 節肢動物/軟骨)

必須掌握嘅要點 (Must-Know Facts)

  • Hyaluronan 透明質酸: Polysaccharide in repair/ECM, needs peptides for attachment.
  • Crosslink: Acrylated HA 丙烯酸化HA with ADH/EDC, then thiol-acrylate 硫醇-丙烯酸酯.
  • RGD addition aids proliferation/migration.
  • Matrigel 基質膠: EHS sarcoma secretion, laminin/collagen/GFs 層粘連蛋白/膠原蛋白/生長因子.
  • For ESCs 胚胎幹細胞: Diluted maintains pluripotency 多能性, undiluted differentiates.
  • Fibrin 纖維蛋白: From fibrinogen/thrombin 纖維蛋白原/凝血酶, aggregates to coagulate.
  • Biocompatible, used as sealant 密封劑, but degrades by fibrinolysis 纖維蛋白溶解.
  • Alginate 藻酸鹽: From algae, hydrophilic, G-blocks crosslink with Ca (egg-carton 蛋盒模型).
  • M-blocks not involved.
  • Chitosan 殼聚糖: From exoskeletons 外骨骼, like GAGs, for cartilage/bone.

Fibrin 纖維蛋白Alginate 藻酸鹽

常見錯誤同考官陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Examiner Traps)

  • 忘記透明質酸需要修飾才能黏附細胞 (Forgetting hyaluronan needs modification for attachment).
  • 混淆藻酸鹽嘅G區同M區 (Mixing G/M blocks in alginate)。

Topic 8 – Synthetic Polymer Scaffolds 合成聚合物支架

大圖景 (Big Picture)

Synthetics offer consistency over naturals, with tunable degradation 可調節降解 for TE.

思維導圖 (Mind map):
Synthetics 合成聚合物 → Less issues 問題較少 (No allergens/Batch var 無過敏原/批次差異) → Examples 例子 (PLA/PGA/PLGA/PCL/PEOT/PBT) → Degradation 降解 (Bulk/Surface 整體/表面) → Apps 應用 (Injectable/Long-term 可注射/長期)

必須掌握嘅要點 (Must-Know Facts)

  • Less byproducts, variations, immunogenicity than naturals.
  • PLA 聚乳酸: Semicrystalline, brittle 脆, hydrophobic 疏水, degrades to lactic acid 乳酸.
  • Copolymerize with PEG for solubility.
  • PGA 聚乙醇酸: Crystalline, tough 堅韌, degrades to glycolic acid 乙醇酸, for sutures 縫合線.
  • PLGA 聚乳酸-乙醇酸共聚物: Copolymers, biocompatible, acidic products inflame.
  • PCL 聚己內酯: Biocompatible, slow bulk erosion 整體侵蝕 (>3 yrs).
  • PEOT/PBT: Elastic, reversible crosslinks, tunable properties.
  • PPEs/PPAs 聚磷酸酯/聚磷酸酯膠: Surface erosion 表面侵蝕, milder response.
  • PPF-DA/PEG-DA 聚富馬酸丙二醇酯-二丙烯酸酯/聚乙二醇-二丙烯酸酯: Injectable, fill defects, low strength.
  • Categories: Porous solid (bone) vs hydrogels (soft tissues).
  • Solid: Stable, non-dissolve, hydrolyze.
  • Hydrogels: Fill defects, minimally invasive, incorporate cells.

對比表 (Comparison Table) - Natural vs Synthetic Polymers (Scaffolds) 天然 vs 合成聚合物 (支架)

Natural 天然Synthetic 合成
Variable/biocompatible 多變/生物相容Consistent/tunable 穩定/可調節
Immunogenic risk 免疫原性風險Less inflammation 炎症較少
From sources 來源於天然Lab-made 實驗室製備

常見錯誤同考官陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Examiner Traps)

  • 認為合成聚合物不可降解 (Thinking synthetics non-biodegradable): 許多是可降解的。
  • 忘記酸性降解產物 (Forgetting acidic degradation products): 可能引起炎症。

Topic 9 – 3D Cell Cultures and Applications 3D 細胞培養與應用

大圖景 (Big Picture)

3D cultures 3D培養 better mimic in vivo 體內 via scaffolds, improving cell behavior over 2D 二維.

思維導圖 (Mind map):
3D vs 2D → Why 原因 (ECM signals/Topography ECM信號/拓撲結構) → Limitations 局限性 (Diffusion 擴散) → Apps 應用 (Regen med/Drug discovery/Bioreactors 再生醫學/藥物發現/生物反應器)

必須掌握嘅要點 (Must-Know Facts)

  • 2D artificial 人造; 3D natural with ECM cues.
  • Control morphology/architecture/components.
  • Cells respond in vivo-like.
  • Matrices: Porous, support growth/differentiation.
  • Chemical mod: Adhesion/degradation/GF release.
  • Limited diffusion 擴散 in 3D; viability drops over time.
  • Apps: Clinical 臨床, in vitro 體外, regen med 再生醫學, drug discovery 藥物發現.
  • Bioreactors 生物反應器: Stirring/perfusion 攪拌/灌注 for mass transport 物質傳輸.
  • Focal adhesions 黏著斑 differ 2D vs 3D.
  • 3D for blood vessels/skin/cartilage 血管/皮膚/軟骨.

Topic 1 – Introduction to Polymers

Big Picture (1 sentence + mind map in text)

Polymers are large molecules essential for tissue engineering scaffolds due to their versatility in mimicking ECM.
Mind map: Polymers → Natural (Wood/Rubber/DNA) → Synthetic (PE/PS/PEG) → Biomedical Suitability (Strength/Biodegradability/Fabrication) → Disadvantages (Deformation/Toxicity/Degradation).

Must-Know Facts (max 15 bullet points)

  • Polymers revolutionized chemistry with major everyday impact (Lord Todd quote).
  • ECM components like collagen, proteins, and GAGs are polymers.
  • Suited for biomedical apps: low/high strength, protein attachment resistance/encouragement.
  • Biodegradable or permanent; fabricable into complex shapes.
  • Disadvantages: lower strength than metals/ceramics, deform over time.
  • May deteriorate during sterilization or degrade catastrophically in body.
  • Release toxic byproducts possible.
  • Natural examples: Wood, Rubber, Leather, Cotton, Wool, Silk, DNA, Proteins.
  • Synthetic examples: Polystyrene, PET, PE, PEG, Kevlar.
  • Polymers in scaffolds mimic ECM for cell support.

Topic 2 – Polymerization Types

Big Picture (1 sentence + mind map in text)

Polymerization forms chains via addition (non-degradable) or step-growth (degradable via hydrolysis).
Mind map: Polymerization → Addition (Radicals/Ions → No labile bonds) → Step-Growth (Condensation → Ester bonds → Hydrolysis) → Examples (PE vs PLA/PGA/PLGA).

Must-Know Facts (max 15 bullet points)

  • Addition: Double-bond monomers like ethylene, vinyl chloride, styrene, MMA.
  • Via radicals or ions; no labile bonds, not degradable.
  • Example: Ethylene → Polyethylene.
  • Step-growth: Functional group reactions, condensation.
  • Well-known: PLA, PGA, PLGA.
  • Degrade via main chain ester hydrolysis.
  • PLA from lactic acid condensation.
  • PGA from glycolic acid.
  • PLGA copolymers tunable.

  • Forgetting condensation byproduct in step-growth.

Mnemonics / Memory Hooks

Topic 3 – Polymer Structures and States

Big Picture (1 sentence + mind map in text)

Polymers exist as crystalline, amorphous, or semi-crystalline, affecting melting and glass transitions.
Mind map: Structures → Crystals (Ordered/Lattice/Sharp Tm) → Amorphous (No order/Tg) → Polymers (Semi-crystalline/Folding/Stacking) → States (Rubber/Glass).

Must-Know Facts (max 15 bullet points)

  • Crystals: Infinite 3D lattice, sharp Tm.
  • Amorphous: No structure, sharp Tm.
  • Small molecules: 100% crystalline or amorphous.
  • Polymer crystalline: Intramolecular folding/stacking.
  • Semi-crystalline: Never 100% crystalline, have Tm.
  • Rubber/Glass: Frozen liquid, no long-range order.
  • Tg: Below Tg, no cooperative rotation/translation.
  • Transition: Rubbery to glassy hard state.
  • Crystallization favored by simple structures, small side groups.
  • Linear, non-crosslinked polymers crystallize better.
  • Block/alternating copolymers, slow cooling aid crystallization.
  • Crystalline: Stronger, resistant to dissolution.

Topic 4 – Polymer Properties

Big Picture (1 sentence + mind map in text)

Properties like crystallinity, thermal behavior, and side groups influence polymer performance in scaffolds.
Mind map: Properties → Crystallinity (Favored by simplicity) → Side Groups (Flexible lower Tg) → Thermal (Tg/Tm values) → Effects (Free volume/Motion).

Must-Know Facts (max 15 bullet points)

  • Crystallization: Simple/small side groups, linear, non-network.
  • Slow cooling for chain motion.
  • Crystalline stronger/resistant.
  • Flexible side groups: Tg decreases with length.
  • Side chains rotate, increase free volume, lower Tg.
  • Thermal examples: Nylon 6,6 Tg 45°C Tm 267°C.
  • UHMWPE Tg -125°C Tm 140°C.
  • Silicone Tg -123°C Tm -29°C.
  • PMMA Tg 105°C Tm 160°C.
  • PDLLA Tg 50°C amorphous.
  • PCL Tg -60°C Tm 57°C.
  • PGA Tg 35°C Tm 210°C.

Crosslinks create networks that swell in water to form hydrogels for cell environments.

  • Crosslinks: Covalent, H-bonding, entanglements.
  • Increase MW, swell in solvents (hydrogel).
  • Hydrogels: Water-swollen crosslinked structures.

  • Why: Cells need aqueous for nutrients/waste.
  • Prepared by crosslinking then swelling.
  • PHEMA common: Tissue-like water, degradation resistant.
  • Apps: Contacts, wounds, kidney membranes.
  • Swelling: Thermodynamic force vs retractive.
  • Highly swollen: Cellulose, PVA, PEG.
  • Moderately: PHEMA.

Topic 8 – Synthetic Polymer Scaffolds

Synthetics offer consistency over naturals, with tunable degradation for TE.

  • Less byproducts, variations, immunogenicity than naturals.
  • PLA: Semicrystalline, brittle, hydrophobic, degrades to lactic acid.
  • Copolymerize with PEG for solubility.
  • PGA: Crystalline, tough, degrades to glycolic acid, for sutures.
  • PLGA: Copolymers, biocompatible, acidic products inflame.

  • PPEs/PPAs: Surface erosion, milder response.
  • PPF-DA/PEG-DA: Injectable, fill defects, low strength.
  • Categories: Porous solid (bone) vs hydrogels (soft tissues).
  • Solid: Stable, non-dissolve, hydrolyze.
  • Hydrogels: Fill defects, minimally invasive, incorporate cells.
